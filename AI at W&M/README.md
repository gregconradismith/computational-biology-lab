# Artificial Intelligence at W&M

This directory collects notes and links related to artificial intelligence resources at William & Mary, with an emphasis on material useful for Computational Biology Lab students.

## Start Here

- [Artificial Intelligence at W&M](https://www.wm.edu/sites/ai/)

The W&M AI site is the central place to look for campus guidance, opportunities, events, and resources related to artificial intelligence.

## Suggested Use

Use this folder for lab notes about AI tools, responsible use in research and coursework, campus policies, workshops, and examples that connect AI methods to computational biology workflows.
